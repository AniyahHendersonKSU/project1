Analyzing the Cafe: An Analysis of HTML5 & CSS in the "Cafe" Template

This site is an educational breakdown of "Cafe" template available on w3schools.com. This project takes a visually appealing website and breaks it into what HTML 5 elements were choosen and why, and how its CSS aspects were introduced.

The goal isn't to praise or dismiss the template, but to understand it well enough to explain its decisions to someone else — including the places where it takes shortcuts, repeats itself, or relies on inline styles instead of the stylesheet doing the work.
